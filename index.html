About Me

Overview

I have been studying a wide range of software development and web development skills over the past 5 to 6 years. Four of those years have been in college refining and expanding my skill set.
Born Ireland and raised in a small town called Arklow, I attended Dun Laoghaire Further Education Institute, starting out with hopes of becoming a hardware engineer but graduated with a burning passion for software and web development. I began pursuing my new found passion in National College of Ireland.
In 2016 I made a terrible mistake of neglecting a module in my degree and focused on subjects I felt were more important. The result I failed by 1%, a bitter pill to swallow but I learned a lot from that mistake and don't believe I would have ever gotten very far past my degree without that failure.
In 2017 I got a job working as a kitchen porter in Amber Springs Hotel and Spa, this is where I learned the true meaning of hard work and perseverance. Now in 2018, I am going back to National College of Ireland to complete my degree bringing everything I have learned over the past two years with me.
